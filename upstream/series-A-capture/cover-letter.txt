Subject: [PATCH] ASoC: tas2783: skip capture when no SoundWire source ports

Hi,

Single patch for tas2783-sdw.c.

Some SoundWire amplifier DisCo properties expose sink ports only. When
such a codec is still referenced on a capture dai_link, stream prepare
fails because no source DPN exists. Skip capture attachment based on
runtime slave properties (source_ports == 0).

Reported-on: ASUS ProArt PX13 (AMD ACP70). No functional change to
playback.
