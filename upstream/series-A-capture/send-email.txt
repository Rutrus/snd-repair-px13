Subject: [PATCH] ASoC: tas2783: skip capture when no SoundWire source ports

Hi,

Some SoundWire amplifier DisCo properties expose sink data ports only.
Machine drivers may still list such a codec on a capture dai_link. The
driver then attempts to join a capture stream and program a source DPN
that does not exist, and prepare fails with -EINVAL.

This patch checks the runtime-discovered slave properties and skips
capture stream attachment when source_ports is zero. Playback is
unchanged.

Reported-on: ASUS ProArt PX13 (AMD ACP70). A longer investigation
report is available on request.
