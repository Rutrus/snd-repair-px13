## Inference (strong)

The bottleneck **after W1** is **TAS2783 lifecycle**, not ACP IRQ delivery:

```text
ATTACHED (bus OK)
    ↓
tas_io_init() never runs (hw_init stale / no series B on module)
    ↓
fw_dl_success stays false
    ↓
hw_params timeout → -EINVAL
```
